WHAT'S THIS ABOUT?

The fruit drive!

The fruit drive is an initiative led by team Zirconia in collaboration with App Idea 2r in a bid to achieve the United Nations Sustainable Development goals, primarily in Pakistan and the ECO region, and ultimately the whole world. It is envisioned via this program to eradicate poverty, reduce hunger and generate employment opportunities for the destitute. The program aims to achieve this by improvising public areas such as car parking, jogging tracks and similar areas for growing organics i.e fruits and vegetables, under its driving philosophy

"Effort from the people; for the people."
    Benefits to the society:
  1. Quality nutrition will be provided to the poor
  2. Creates Jobs: when trees will be allotted to poor people along with loaned shops, they could startup their own business
  3. Assists government in understanding problems associated with poor people: our app could provide micro-details that are often neglected while focusing on major challenges
  4. Reduces distances between classes: The crops will be grown from the efforts of the privileged for the underprivileged
  5. Problems can be promptly addressed and worked upon: If someone finds a poor locality, via the app he could promptly send basic information such as address. Our app would inform interested organizations and finance their activities.
    6. This drive is a four stage program. Stages include:
  6. Fund generation and resource allocation
  7. Growing crops and its distribution amongst the poor
  8. Opening a shop for the owner
  9. Convincing government officials to include this program as a part of their governing policy.
